My UX Skills

User Research

I believe User Research is the foundation of good design. In-depth, user research is key to determining what features need to be added or removed from a design. Personally, before I make any design decisions, I collect and analyze user research from a minimum of 5 user-personas. Making sure that the proper research is completed at the beginning of a development cycle is a non-negotiable, and is the difference between a successful design, and a failed design.

Prototyping

Quick, frequent prototyping helps keep a design from veering off course. It gives the developer a small glimpse of the overall design, providing opportunities for steady iteration throughout the development cycle. Prototyping saves the developer from scope creep and a need entire redesigns. It takes multiple iterations of prototypes and user-testing feedback to make proper design choices.
