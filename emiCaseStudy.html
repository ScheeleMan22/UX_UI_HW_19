Expressive Movement Initiative

Giving children with disabilities a chance to dance.

empathy, information architecture, connection

The project.
My role.

EMI has been providing dance courses for children with disabiliteies for the last 4 years. Their mission is to create an inclusive learning environment for all children to excell in.

As a UX designer on this project, it was my job to follow a clear development cycle to guide our website redesign from user research, through ideation, and all the way to the final design.
