The project.
My role.

Urban Peak has been fighting back against youth homeslessness in the Denver-metro area for 10 years. When starting Urban Peak back in 2010, it was Niles Cranes mission to have ____ by 2020. Now, moving into the next decade of Urban Peak, the information heirarchy and logic flow of Urban Peak’s webpage needed to be updated for the modern volunteer. And boy, did we provide an update!

As a UX designer on this project, it was my job to follow a clear development cycle to guide our website redesign from user research, through ideation, and all the way to the final design.
